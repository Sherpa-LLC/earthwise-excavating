## Overview

Southwest Ohio's storms can hit hard — high winds, heavy rain, ice, and tornadic activity can leave properties damaged and inaccessible in a matter of hours. When a storm strikes your property, Earthwise Excavating provides the rapid-response excavation and cleanup services you need to get things back to normal.

Our heavy equipment can handle what pickup trucks and chainsaws can't: massive fallen trees, washed-out embankments, collapsed culverts, and blocked access roads. We work quickly and efficiently to clear the damage, restore drainage, and protect your property from further deterioration.

## What's Included

- **Rapid Assessment** — We get to your property quickly to evaluate the damage and prioritize the most critical work.
- **Tree & Debris Clearing** — Heavy equipment removes large fallen trees, branches, and structural debris that blocks access and threatens structures.
- **Driveway & Road Restoration** — We rebuild washed-out drives, clear blocked roads, and restore access to your property.
- **Drainage Repair** — Damaged culverts, collapsed ditches, and blocked drainage systems are repaired or replaced to prevent ongoing water damage.
- **Erosion Stabilization** — We grade and stabilize areas damaged by water erosion to prevent the damage from spreading.

## Our Process

1. **Emergency Contact** — Call us at (513) 212-7585. We prioritize storm calls and will get a crew to you as fast as possible.
2. **Damage Assessment** — We evaluate the full scope of damage and create an action plan, addressing safety hazards first.
3. **Cleanup & Restoration** — Our crew clears debris, restores access, repairs drainage, and stabilizes any erosion damage.
4. **Documentation & Prevention** — We provide documentation for insurance claims and recommend improvements to reduce future storm vulnerability.
