## Overview

Water goes where gravity takes it — and when it goes the wrong way, the damage to your property can be severe and expensive. Earthwise Excavating provides comprehensive drainage and erosion control solutions that manage water effectively while protecting the natural landscape.

Serving Clarksville, Wilmington, Hillsboro, and communities across Southwest Ohio, we design and install drainage systems that solve problems at their source. Whether you're dealing with a wet basement, eroding hillside, flooded driveway, or standing water in your pasture, we have the expertise and equipment to fix it.

## What's Included

- **Site Assessment** — We evaluate water flow patterns, soil types, slopes, and existing drainage infrastructure to diagnose the problem.
- **Custom Drainage Design** — Every property is different. We design solutions tailored to your specific conditions and budget.
- **French Drains & Curtain Drains** — Subsurface drainage systems that intercept and redirect groundwater before it causes problems.
- **Surface Grading & Swales** — Regrading and earthen channels that direct surface water away from structures and sensitive areas.
- **Erosion Repair & Prevention** — Stabilizing damaged slopes, installing erosion blankets, and seeding to restore and protect exposed soil.

## Our Process

1. **Free Property Assessment** — We walk your property to identify drainage issues, water flow patterns, and the root cause of any erosion.
2. **Solution Design & Estimate** — We present a clear plan with materials, timeline, and cost — no guessing.
3. **Expert Installation** — Our crew installs your drainage system with precision, ensuring proper grades and connections throughout.
4. **Restoration & Follow-Up** — We restore disturbed areas with grading and seeding, then check back to confirm everything is performing as designed.
