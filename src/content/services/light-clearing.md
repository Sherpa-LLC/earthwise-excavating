## Overview

Whether you're preparing a building site, reclaiming overgrown pasture, creating fire breaks, or simply opening up a view, Earthwise Excavating provides efficient light clearing that gets your land ready while respecting the natural environment.

Our conservation-focused approach means we don't just clear everything in sight. We work with you to identify what stays and what goes, preserving mature trees, natural drainage features, and the character of your property while removing the brush, saplings, and undergrowth that's in the way.

## What's Included

- **Property Walkthrough** — We identify the clearing boundaries, note trees and features to preserve, and plan equipment access.
- **Brush & Sapling Removal** — Efficient removal of undergrowth, small trees, and invasive vegetation using compact track equipment.
- **Debris Management** — Cleared material is stacked, chipped, or hauled away depending on your preference and local regulations.
- **Surface Cleanup** — We leave the cleared area clean and ready for its next use, not a tangled mess of stumps and debris.
- **Optional Seeding** — We can seed cleared areas to stabilize soil and establish grass cover to prevent erosion.

## Our Process

1. **On-Site Walkthrough** — We review the area, flag trees to keep, and discuss your goals for the cleared land.
2. **Clear Estimate** — You receive a written quote covering scope, timeline, and debris handling.
3. **Careful Clearing** — Our operators work methodically, preserving what matters and clearing what doesn't.
4. **Site Restoration** — We grade and clean the area, and optionally seed for erosion control and a finished appearance.
