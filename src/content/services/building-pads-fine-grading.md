## Overview

A building pad is where your project begins — and getting it right determines everything that follows. At Earthwise Excavating, we specialize in creating precisely graded, properly compacted building pads that give your construction project the stable foundation it needs.

Whether you're building a new home on a rural Clarksville lot, adding a pole barn on your Wilmington farm, or preparing a commercial site near Hillsboro, our crew brings the equipment, expertise, and attention to detail to get it done right the first time.

## What's Included

- **Site Evaluation & Planning** — We assess soil conditions, drainage patterns, and elevation requirements before any dirt moves.
- **Rough Grading** — Our operators establish the proper contour and elevation, moving earth to match your building plans.
- **Fine Grading** — Precision grading brings the pad to exact specifications for foundation placement, flatwork, and proper water runoff.
- **Compaction** — Proper compaction prevents settling and ensures long-term structural stability.
- **Drainage Consideration** — Every pad we build accounts for surface water management to protect your investment.

## Our Process

1. **Free On-Site Consultation** — We walk the property with you, review plans, and assess soil and terrain conditions.
