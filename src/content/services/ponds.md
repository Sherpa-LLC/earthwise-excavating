## Overview

A well-built pond adds beauty, function, and value to any rural property. Whether you want a fishing pond, a livestock watering source, an irrigation reservoir, or a scenic feature that enhances your acreage, Earthwise Excavating has the experience to design and build it right.

We've been building ponds across Southwest Ohio for over two decades. Our knowledge of local soil conditions, hydrology, and Ohio's regulatory landscape means your pond will hold water, function as intended, and enhance your land for years to come.

## What's Included

- **Site Assessment** — We evaluate soil type, topography, watershed area, and groundwater conditions to determine the best pond location and design.
- **Design & Sizing** — We work with you to determine the right size, depth, and shape based on your intended use and property characteristics.
- **Excavation** — Our experienced operators dig the pond basin with proper depth profiles, shelves, and slopes for safety and function.
- **Dam & Spillway Construction** — When applicable, we build properly compacted dams with engineered spillways to safely handle overflow.
- **Finish Grading & Seeding** — We grade the banks, establish proper slopes, and seed disturbed areas for erosion control and a natural appearance.

## Our Process

1. **Free Site Evaluation** — We visit your property, test soil conditions, and discuss your vision for the pond.
2. **Design & Estimate** — We present a design plan with dimensions, estimated fill time, and a clear cost breakdown.
3. **Expert Construction** — Our crew excavates and shapes the pond with precision, building proper dams, spillways, and bank profiles.
4. **Restoration & Guidance** — We restore the surrounding area with grading and seeding, and advise you on pond management for the first year.
